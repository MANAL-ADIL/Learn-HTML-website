Subscript Tag:

This tag is used to define subscript text. Subscript text appears smaller and lower than the surrounding text. It is often used in mathematical formulas and chemical equations. Means ke agr apko koi chemical equation likhni h jese CO2 to ap uska 2 line se neeche likhna chahte h to ap 2 ko sub script tag me likhein ge aur subscript tag ko hm as a tag <sub></sub> likhtay hain.

EXAMPLE:

CO<sub>2</sub>=CO2

Super script Tag:

Agr hm koi b math ki term ya kuch b likh rhe ho or hme value power me chahiye to hm super script tag ka istemal karte hain. Super script tag ko hm as a tag <sup></sup> likhtay hain.

EXAMPLE:

7<sup>2</sup>=7 2 = 49

Video Tag:

The <video> tag is used to embed video content in a web page. It allows you to specify multiple sources for the video, and the browser will choose the best one it can play. The <video> tag also provides built-in controls for play, pause, and volume.It is used when u have video in your file or folder.

EXAMPLE:

<video src="file name" controls loop > </video>

Video tag attributes:

controls attribute adds video controls like play, pause, and volume.
loop attributte se video cycle me chlti h means jese hi end ho dubara chl jaye.
muted attributte se video bina sound ke chlta h.
autoplay attributte se video bina play button ke chlta h lkn chrome isko support nahi karta q ke user experience ko kharab kar sakta h.Agr apko autoplay chahiye to apko iske sath muted attribute b dalna hoga.
